About the Project

Welcome! This is a web app designed as a final project for GEOG 481 - Geographic Information Systems Project at the University of Waterloo.

What?

A web app about quantifying the effects of post-secondary students on sustainable development using a temporal analysis of Waterloo Region, Ontario, Canada.

How?

Using both spatial and non-spatial data, the analysis considers the three pillars of sustainable development - environment, economy and society. Data sources include the Canadian census, and the Municipality of Waterloo.

Why?

Waterloo is growing! It also has a bunch of universities and colleges! It's important to consider what types of initiatives will be needed in the future to continue growing!
